Common repairs

What we fix most

Screen Replacement

Cracked, flickering, or blank screens — laptop and desktop monitor screens replaced.

Keyboard Repair

Sticky, unresponsive or missing keys — full keyboard replacement or individual key fixes.

Battery Replacement

Laptop won't hold charge or drains fast — battery testing and replacement.

Motherboard Repair

Won't power on, random shutdowns, or no display — component-level board repair.

Liquid Damage Recovery

Spilled water, juice or coffee — cleaning, drying and component-level repair.

Slow Computer / Virus Removal

Cleaning malware, removing junk software, and speeding up a sluggish machine.

Data Recovery

Recovering files from drives that won't boot, are corrupted, or were accidentally formatted.

Networking Issues

No internet, slow WiFi, or printer not connecting — full network troubleshooting.

Maintenance, not just repair

Stop problems before they start

Dust, heat and Kigali's variable power supply wear down computers faster than normal use does. Our maintenance visits catch the early warning signs — a fan working too hard, a battery losing capacity, a hard drive showing errors — before they turn into a full breakdown.

  • Internal dust cleaning and thermal paste renewal
  • Operating system and security updates
  • Battery and storage health checks
  • Network and printer connectivity checks
