About Prosper Technology Ltd

A Kigali-based team that sells computers and accessories, and keeps them running long after the sale.

Our story

Started by people who fix things, not just sell them

Prosper Technology Ltd was founded in Kigali by technicians who got tired of seeing customers sold computers with no support afterward. We built the business the other way around: we are repair people first, and that's why we know what to sell, and to whom.

Today we operate from Kigali town, opposite Adper Nyarugenge — selling new and quality pre-owned computers and accessories, and running a full repair and maintenance workshop for individuals, schools, NGOs and small businesses across the city.
